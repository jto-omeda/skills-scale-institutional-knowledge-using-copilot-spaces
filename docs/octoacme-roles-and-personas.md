# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Agile Coaches guide teams in adopting and improving Agile practices. They mentor team members in continuous improvement, facilitate Agile ceremonies, and partner with Project and Delivery Managers to embed best practices and resolve process impediments.

### Responsibilities
- Coach teams on Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban, etc.)
- Facilitate retrospectives and improvement experiments
- Help identify and remove process impediments
- Mentor Project Managers and team leads in Agile practices
- Support teams in self-organization and continuous improvement
- Track and share metrics on team health and delivery efficiency

### Goals
- Improve team velocity and predictability
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and learning
- Reduce process friction and waste
- Increase team autonomy and engagement

### Typical Communication
- Retrospective facilitation and action item tracking
- Coaching sessions with teams and individuals
- Metrics dashboards and improvement progress reports
- Collaboration with Project Managers on process refinement

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Managers**: Partners on delivery process optimization, shares insights on team health
- **Developers**: Coaches on Agile practices, helps with estimation and planning techniques
- **Product Managers**: Collaborates on backlog refinement practices and feedback loops

---

## Compliance Lead

### Role Summary
Compliance Leads ensure projects meet regulatory standards, industry requirements, and internal policies. They conduct compliance reviews, support risk mitigation efforts, and coordinate with PMO, Product Owners, and stakeholders to maintain adherence to applicable standards.

### Responsibilities
- Review project plans for compliance with regulations and standards
- Conduct compliance audits and risk assessments
- Provide guidance on regulatory requirements and best practices
- Maintain compliance documentation and audit trails
- Coordinate with legal, security, and governance teams
- Support incident response for compliance-related issues

### Goals
- Ensure all projects meet regulatory and policy requirements
- Minimize compliance-related risks and incidents
- Maintain clear audit trails and documentation
- Educate teams on compliance requirements proactively

### Typical Communication
- Compliance review reports and risk assessments
- Regular check-ins with PMO and Project Managers
- Guidance documents and training materials
- Stakeholder updates on compliance status

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Managers**: Reviews project plans, provides compliance guidance and checkpoints
- **Product Managers**: Advises on regulatory constraints that impact product features
- **Developers**: Provides compliance requirements for implementation (security, data handling, etc.)

---

## Stakeholder Engagement Manager

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Engagement Managers proactively communicate with project stakeholders, gather feedback, manage expectation alignment, and bridge between delivery teams and sponsors. They ensure stakeholders remain informed and engaged throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Identify and map stakeholder groups and communication needs
- Create and execute stakeholder communication plans
- Gather stakeholder feedback and requirements
- Manage stakeholder expectations and alignment
- Facilitate stakeholder meetings and presentations
- Escalate stakeholder concerns and ensure resolution

### Goals
- Maintain high stakeholder satisfaction and engagement
- Ensure timely and transparent communication
- Prevent surprises and manage expectations proactively
- Build strong relationships between teams and stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Regular stakeholder updates and newsletters
- Presentation decks for executive briefings
- Feedback collection and analysis reports
- Escalation notifications and resolution tracking

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Managers**: Coordinates on stakeholder communications, provides stakeholder feedback
- **Product Managers**: Gathers stakeholder input on priorities and requirements
- **Developers**: Translates technical updates into stakeholder-friendly communications
